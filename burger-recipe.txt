Ingredients:
 - 2 lbs 80/20 ground beef
 - salt 
 - pepper 

Directions:
 1. Gently form 1/4 lb patties and season one side generously with salt and pepper (8 patties total) 
 2. Place patty season-side down on the cast iron for 5 minutes (if on 5 heat) 
 3. Season the top side of the burger and flip
 4. Check temperature after 4 minutes
